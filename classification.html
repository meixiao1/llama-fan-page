Llamas Scientific Name and Classification

Common Name Llamas
Scientific Name Lama glama

Scientific Classification Details

Llamas belong to the family Camelidae, which also includes other camelids such as alpacas, guanacos, and vicu√±as. They are classified as follows:

  • Kingdom: Animalia (animals)
  • Phylum: Chordata (vertebrates)
  • Class: Mammalia (mammals)
  • Order: Artiodactyla (even-toed ungulates)
  • Family: Camelidae (camels and relatives)
  • Genus: Lama
  • Species: Lama glama

Llamas are domesticated South American camelids known for their wool and use as pack animals. The wild ancestor of the llama is the guanaco (Lama guanicoe), which is found in the wild in South America.
