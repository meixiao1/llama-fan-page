Fun Facts About Llamas

  • Llamas are social animals and prefer to live with other llamas as a herd.
  • They can carry loads of up to 30% of their body weight for several miles.
  • Llamas have been used as pack animals by Andean cultures since pre-Hispanic times.
  • They are very curious and will often approach people to investigate.
  • Llamas communicate with each other through a series of ear, body, and tail postures, as well as vocalizations.
